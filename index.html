Deadpool

Deadpool's healing factor not only allows him to recover from injuries quickly but also slows his aging, making him virtually immortal. He is skilled in hand-to-hand combat and proficient with various weapons, often wielding katanas and firearms.The character gained mainstream popularity with the release of the 2016 film "Deadpool," starring Ryan Reynolds. The film's success led to a sequel, "Deadpool 2," further establishing Deadpool as a fan-favorite in both comic books and cinema
